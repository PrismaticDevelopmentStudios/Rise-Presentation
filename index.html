Matthew Brignola On His Journey

My name is Matthew Brignola. I am a 29 year old male who incurred a traumatic brain injury while downhill mountain biking at Winter Park Mountain Resort on September 2, 2019. I was initially hospitalized at Winter Park Health and then helicoptered to Denver Health. I was luckily responsive within the first week I was in a coma and was transferred to Craig Hospital to begin the inpatient program. I was able to be in Craig's outpatient program until the program was haulted because of the Covid-19 pandemic. I went to live with my mother and step father at their home in Colorado Springs and continued doing outpatient services at Penrose Outpatient Rehebilitation.

Life for me has definitely not resumed to how it was before my injury, but I am making sure top take steps to help me reachieve fincancial independence.I am currently taking an online course through the University of Denver in order to recieve a certificate of completion so I can become a web developer. Outlined in the "Goals" page is a description of my intensions in taking this course, as well as the Humen services resources I used to pay for my scholarship.
